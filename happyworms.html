happy worms

Creating a positive brand that will encourage sustainabilty, approaching life with intention and peace.

Happy Worms was a concept that I conceived during a visit to this old man to purchase worms. I tried contacting a few locations on the island to see if they had worms before finding this guy’s backyard business. As a result, I created this fictitious company for composting worms.

Services: Print Design, Typesetting
Software: Procreate, PhotoShop, Illustrator, InDesign, XD, HTML, CSS, Lottie

creative process

Discovery

In the discovery phase, I aim to solidify the goals for the brand identity. Understanding the long-term objectives and needs for the brand will help me to inform the creative approach and technical solutions.

Ideation

Using the brand strategy as a guide, ideation through sketching will uncover potential visual and conceptual design solutions. These are in the form of assets—logo/tagline, colors, typography, imagery, (graphics, icons, patterns and/or textures.)

Design

Utilizing feedback from the ideation phase, the chosen design direction is then revised to meet project objectives.

Final Result

The final outcome was a brand identity that reflects our goal for encouraging sustainability and healthy lifestyles and habits.
