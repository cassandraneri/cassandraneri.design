creative process

Discovery

In the discovery phase, I aim to establish my brand strategy, which includes who the target audience is and what the brand's mission and values are. This will help me in the ideation phase to generate strong concepts for my logo for Happy Worms.

01

Ideation

After doing lots of research on composting worms, I learned an interesting fact that lead my creative approach for my logo concept.

02

Design

I digitized my concept and then worked on developing the rest of my assets for the brand including the colors, typography, and textures. Utilizing feedback from the ideation phase, I then revised my inital designs to meet project objectives.

03

Final Result

The final outcome was a brand identity that reflects the sustainable, innovative, and positive nature of our 3 exceptional kitchen products.
