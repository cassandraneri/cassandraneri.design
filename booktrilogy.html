book trilogy design

Designing book covers for three of David Sedaris's ingenious short stories.

My creative process began with the initial cover design for the first book in my trilogy Untamed, which dealt with making friends with animals. I then repeated the process to design the covers for the second and third books in the trilogy, taking into consideration cohesion and viability for a trilogy.

Services: Print Design, Typesetting
Software: InDesign, Procreate, Photoshop, After Effects, Premiere Pro

my creative process:

Discovery

The goal of creating a book cover is to entice the consumer to want to explore the book. The design should represent the story without giving away too many details.

01

Ideation

As a part of my research, I first, read the story online, establishing a tone for the story, as well as what the main themes/ideas were that I was trying to discover. After spending some time brainstorming, sketching, and resketching, I eventually came up with strong concepts for my book cover after taking note of many weak ideas and resketching them many more times.

02

Design

Bringing my concepts to life involved experimenting with different mediums, including paint, digital software, and air-hardening clay.

03

Final Result

The final outcome was a set of three cohesive paintings used to create the trilogy's book covers to a few of David Sedaris's stories.
